About

Bridging the gap between AI engineering education and real-world business impact.

Our Mission

Most AI courses teach algorithms in isolation. You learn how a neural network works, but not how to deploy it to predict customer churn. You understand attention mechanisms, but not how to build a document search system for a law firm.

AI Systems Engineering in Business bridges that gap. Every concept is taught through a business lens — ROI, customer impact, and strategic value. You don't just learn what; you learn why and when.

The curriculum is built on a simple philosophy: build from scratch first, then use frameworks. Implement gradient descent by hand before touching PyTorch. Write a tokenizer before using Hugging Face. This deep understanding is what separates engineers who configure models from those who create them.

Built On

This curriculum is a complete redesign and expansion of AI Engineering from Scratch by Rohit Ghumare. We've taken the solid technical foundation and wrapped it in:

  • Business context for every algorithm
  • Interactive browser-based code execution (Pyodide)
  • Progress tracking and quizzes
  • A modern, playful web experience
  • Blog and glossary for ongoing learning
